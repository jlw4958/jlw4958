About Me


Hello! My name is Jabrecia Washington. I am a Game Design & Development major at Rochester Institute of Technology. I am a storyteller at heart and aim to share these stories through the world through whatever medium fits them best. Games, I feel, are a perfect way to immerse an individual in a story and ensure they get the most out of it. I am also interested in web development, 3D design, 2D design, and UI/UX design. When I'm not coding the day away, I'm either singing, drawing, or writing. As an artistic mind, I appreciate the intersectionality between technology and art that game development brings.

Project 1

I created Clouds of Personality as an idea spurred by the prompt for this year, "cloud." I have alwasys had a love for clouds and wanted to take the opportunity to practice my web design skills. Creating this project really allowed me to learn more about myself as a web designer and realize what I still had left to learn. The project recieved lots of praise from all who experienced it, and I plan to improve upon the website more as I grow and increase my skills. Above is a link to the website so you can try it out yourself!

Project 2

This project was an assignment to learn more about how to utilize and implement APIs. I enjoyed the chance to improve my web development and JavaScript skills. This assignment recieved a lot of positive feedback, and I plan to improve it more in the future to allow the user to only choose on criteria, display the results in a more interactive and visually pleasing manner, and to make the overall site more enjoyable for users.
